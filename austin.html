Austin, Texas population stands at around 950,000 people, making it one of the fastest -growing cities in the United States! The city was originally incorporated as a city on December 27th, 1839 in the Texas Hill County with a natural beauty and urban vibrance. The average income in Austin is typically higher than the state average sitting at $41K for individuals, and $75k for households. This is due to Austin's tech sector and creative industry, making it the highest income average in the state.

With a rich history, a diverse population, and a unique blend of urban and natural beauty, Austin continues to be a beacon of innovation, culture, and inspiration in the Lone Star State.
