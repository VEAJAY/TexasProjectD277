San Antonio, Texas is a city of history and tradition, being the home of 1.5 million residents becoming the seventh-largest city in the US and the second-largest city in the state. The now urban city was incorporated on June 5th, 1837, located near the Gulf of Mexico. San Antonio's income average comes from a mixture of industries, ranging from $20K to as high as $150,000K, but falls slighlty below the state average at roughly $50K for households, ranking the lowest in the state.
