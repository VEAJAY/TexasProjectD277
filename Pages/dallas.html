Nestled in the heart of the Lone Star State, Dallas, Texas, is a city of boundless energy, rich history, and a thriving blend of cultures. The city is home to approximately 1.3 million residents, making it the third-largest city in Texas. Sitting in the North Texas region, this urban city was eastablished on Februrary 2, 1856. With its robust job opportunities through technonlogy, healthcare, and finance industries, Dallas average income for individuals is roughly $32K. The city is also known for its popular sports teams such as:

  • The Dallas Cowboys football team
  • The Dallas Mavericks basketball team
  • The Dallas Stars ice hockey team
