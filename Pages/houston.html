Located in the southeastern part of Texas, Houston is a city of diversity and boundless opportunities. Houston is home to 2.3 million people making it the fourth-largest city in the US and the largest city in the state. Houston became a city officially on June 5th, 1837, and through the decades became a sprawling urban city. The average income for an individual in Houston is around $30.4K with a diversity of industries such as aerospace and manufacturing. The city is also known for its infamous baseball team.
